An initiative by Dainik Jagran to foster informed citizenship and develop the next generation of public leaders through structured, high-impact parliamentary simulation.

Jagran Youth Parliament recognizes that India's most complex challenges require fresh perspectives, not recycled solutions. JYP is a platform designed to immerse India's brightest minds in exploring ideas through democratic process. Through structured debates and interactive simulations, JYP builds leadership, develops communication skills, and inspires civic engagement.

Eligibility & Rules

Jagran Youth Parliament 2025

  1. Only Under Graduates and Post Graduates students of recognized University/College can apply.
  2. Applicants need to apply in team of two.
  3. Applicants (Partners) have to fill the information in the same form as participant 1 and participant 2.
  4. All fields of form are mandatory to fill.
  5. Only valid college I-card must be uploaded as proof of identity.
  6. The team has to select from the given motion for the debate.
  7. Member of each team has to debate for and against the motion.
  8. Applicants have to choose one preferred language from Hindi or English.
  9. Registration confirmation will be shared on the email IDs provided by the applicants.
  10. A pair can register one time only.
  11. Debate competition will be held at college/university level.
  12. The winners will be decided by the jury as a team and not as individuals.
  13. Selected team will be informed through email.

Our Mission

To empower youth with the knowledge, confidence, and leadership skills to actively engage in India’s democratic process and become the voices of progressive change.

How JYP Works

Experience the structure and depth of real parliamentary simulations designed to shape impactful leaders.

Debate and Collaborate

6 minutes paired debates at UG & PG level,
topic-based argumentation, judged on articulation and logic.

Develop Skills

30-day online training on critical thinking, policy understanding, teamwork, and diplomacy.

Role Play as Parliamentarians

Take roles of MPs & Ministers and engage in real issue debates during 2-day JYP session in Delhi with policymakers.

Program Flow

Phase 1
  • Choosing a partner for team debate
  • Registration on website
  • Selection of Topic
Phase 2
  • UG: Participate in college & inter-college debate
  • PG: Participate in department & interdepartment debate
  • Winners will be selected by jury
  • Only winners will be eligible to be the part of JYP
Phase 3
  • 15 days training on Parliamentary procedures
  • 15 days training on various Bills and Laws
Phase 4
  • Selected JYP members to participate in 2-day session in Delhi
  • Debate and discussion in presence of policy makers
